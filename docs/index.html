COVID-19: US State-level small multiples

Here's showing several ways of small multiples of the COVID-19 cases in the US state-level.

Chart 1: Datetime, Same Y Axis

This chart is basic small multiples, with dates as X axis and same Y axis.

Chart 2: Days after the first case, Same Y Axis

This chart is small multiples, with the days after the first case as X axis and same Y axis.

Chart 3: Datetime, Different Y Axis

This chart is small multiples, with dates as X axis and different Y axis.

Chart 4: Days after the first case, Different Y Axis

This chart is small multiples, with the days after the first case as X axis and different Y axis.
